Achievacity

For helping people achieve their goals

What is Achievacity?

It's a web app that daily scores people's success with their goals—any goals.

Why Achievacity?

It's simple and straight-to-the-point. It's also as open-source as possible.

How can I get started with Achievacity?

Unfortunately, this app hasn't been released yet. If you are a developer who would like to get involved, check out the project's GitHub repo, where you can contribute!
